Nutrition Facts

Fried chicken is a rich source of protein, essential for muscle growth and repair. The flour coating provides carbohydrates for energy, while the frying oil contributes fats for flavor and satiety. Using moderate oil and draining properly reduces excess fat. Seasonings like paprika, garlic, and onion powder add flavor without additional calories.
